About NCICDLA'25

Overview

" National Conference on Innovative Challenges in Deep Learning and its applications " revolves systems, machines and devices that possess a certain degree of intelligence with the capacity to gather and analyse data and offer around diverse Domains.

Our Goal

The NationalConference on Innovative Challenges in Deep Learning and its applications (NCICDLA-25) aims to bring together researchers in related fields to explore and discuss various aspects of intelligent systems and Deep Learning applications.
