KKR & KSR Institute of Technology and Sciences

KKR & KSR INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY AND SCIENCES (KITS) was established in 2008 by the GSR & KKR Educational Society in Vinjanampadu village, Vatticherukuru Mandal, Guntur district, under the visionary leadership of Sri Koye Subba Rao Garu.


Currently, the institute is one of the top engineering colleges in Andhra Pradesh, providing quality technical education to its students. The institute has set a benchmark in offering state-of-the-art infrastructure and experienced, dedicated faculty. It meticulously implements a quality policy with absolute discipline.


The campus features well-ventilated classrooms, multimodal teaching methodologies, campus placement training, caring teachers, committed staff, a proactive managing committee, vigilant security, a well-furnished canteen, a fleet of buses, a mineral water plant, a spacious auditorium, outdoor grounds, and power backup with three generators, among other amenities.


KITS is a unique professional college in Andhra Pradesh, permanently affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada. The institution is approved by AICTE and accredited by NAAC with an 'A' Grade and NBA. In recognition of its excellence, the college was granted autonomous status by the UGC in 2020.
